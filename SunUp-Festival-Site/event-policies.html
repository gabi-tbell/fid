● Health & Safety Policies ●

At our music festival, we strive to provide an enjoyable and safe experience for all attendees. As such, we have established the following event policies:



  1. ﻿Alcohol Policy:
    • Alcoholic beverages will be served at the festival but only to attendees over 21 years old.
    • ID checks will be strictly enforced at all times.
    • Intoxicated attendees will be removed from the festival grounds immediately


  2. Drug Policy:﻿
    • Possession, distribution or use of illegal substances will not be tolerated at the festival.﻿
    • Attendees caught with illegal substances will be escorted off the premises and turned over to the authorities.
    • Attendees are prohibited from bringing weapons or any dangerous items to the festival.﻿


  3. Safety Policy:
    • Backpacks and bags will be thoroughly searched at the entrance gate to ensure the safety of all attendees.﻿


  4. Photography Policy:
    • Attendees are allowed to take photos or record videos for personal use only.﻿
    • Professional photography equipment and drones are prohibited without prior permission.


  5. Respect Policy:
    • We expect all attendees to respect each other and our performers.﻿
    • Any kind of discriminatory or abusive behavior will not be tolerated and will lead to immediate expulsion from the festival.


  6. Lost and Found Policy:
    • We will have a designated Lost and Found area where attendees can retrieve lost items.﻿
    • All found items will be turned over to the authorities if not claimed within a week after the festival.﻿


We want all of our attendees to have a great time while enjoying our music festival, but safety is our top priority. Please follow our event policies to help us ensure a safe and fun environment for all.





● Accommodations ●

We strive to make sure everyone has the opportunity to enjoy the event regardless of any accessibility concerns. We have created the following accommodations to ensure a positive and inclusive experience for all attendees:



  1. ﻿Accessible Transportation:
    • We provide shuttle buses to and from the festival for attendees with mobility issues. Our shuttle buses are wheelchair accessible and can accommodate service animals.


  2. Seating and Viewing Areas:
    • We have designated accessible seating and viewing areas throughout the festival grounds. These areas are designed for wheelchair users and attendees who may require additional space.


  3. Sign Language Interpretation:
    • We offer American Sign Language (ASL) interpretation for all performances on our main stage. ASL interpreters will also be available at designated information booths.


  4. Service Animals:
    • We welcome all service animals on our festival grounds. Service animals must be under the control of their handlers at all times.


  5. Accommodation Requests:
    • We are committed to accommodating all requests for accessibility concerns. If you require any special accommodations, please contact us in advance so we can make the necessary arrangements.


  6. Accessibility Information:
    • We provide comprehensive accessibility information on our website and at the festival information booths. This information includes accessible parking, accessible restrooms, and other accessibility accommodations.



At our music festival, we believe that everyone deserves to enjoy live music without any accessibility barriers. We are committed to providing an inclusive and welcoming environment for all attendees.
