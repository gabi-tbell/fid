Bon Appétit! Time to Eat!

Welcome to Kebab & Spice, the Asian restaurant that promises a gastronomical adventure you'll never forget!

Our restaurant was founded in 2012 with the vision of introducing the diverse flavors of Asian cuisine to the food-loving community.

Our menu boasts of a vast selection of dishes that caters to both vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike.

At Kebab & Spice, we prioritize quality above all else. Stop by our food stall! We hope that we have piqued your interest and hope to see you soon!

Welcome to our family-owned Mexican restaurant! Here at the Madre Taqueria, we specialize in serving up the most mouth-watering, reinspired Mexican cuisine and all kinds of delicious tacos.

Our passion for Mexican cuisine began in our grandmother's kitchen, where we were first introduced to the rich and vibrant flavors that have come to define our culture. Our goal is to share that passion with you, through every dish that we create.

Our tacos are made with the freshest ingredients, sourced from local suppliers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

But we're more than just a restaurant – we're a family.

From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be welcomed with open arms and treated like one of our own.

We believe that food is more than just nourishment; it's an experience that brings people together and creates lasting memories.

So whether you're looking to savor some of the best tacos in town, or you simply want to bask in the warmth and hospitality of a family-owned business, we invite you to join us.

Our table is your table, and we can't wait to share our passion for Mexican cuisine with you.

Welcome to The Alternative Cow! We are thrilled to be the new and up and coming spot for young foodies in the area.

Our menu has many options that can satisfy everyone’s taste buds. Our menu features turkey burgers and plant-based burgers, alongside the traditional beef burgers. Whether you’re vegan, vegetarian, or just want a healthy alternative to the usual fast food, we have got you covered! Aside from burgers, we have a variety of sides and drinks. From crispy sweet potato fries to refreshing homemade lemonade, there’s something for everyone at our place.

Thank you for choosing us. We hope to see you soon!

Welcome to our Wafles food truck, where we serve up some of the most delicious and innovative waffles around!

From sweet to savory, we have something to suit every craving, and our selection of sauces and toppings is sure to get your taste buds buzzing.

At our food truck, we believe that food is more than just sustenance - it's an experience, a source of pleasure and delight that brings people together. That's why we're passionate about creating waffles that are not only Instagram-worthy, but that taste amazing too. Each of our recipes is crafted with care, using only the finest ingredients to create a perfect balance of flavor and texture.

Whether you're in the mood for something sweet, like our classic Belgian waffle topped with fresh berries and whipped cream, or something savory, like our bacon and cheddar waffle with a side of maple syrup, we've got you covered. And with a range of toppings like Nutella, caramel, and peanut butter, you can customize your waffle just the way you like it.

We also believe that good food should be accessible to everyone, which is why we're proud to offer vegan and gluten-free options that don't sacrifice flavor. Our waffles are made with rice and coconut flour and almond milk to create a vegan version that is both delicious and nutritious.

So whether you're on the go and need a quick bite, or you're looking for a fun and unique catering option for your next event, come and check out our Waffle food truck! With great taste and amazing toppings, we guarantee that you'll fall in love with our waffles just as much as we have.
