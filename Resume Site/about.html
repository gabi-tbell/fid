About Me
From humble beginnings, my journey to working in Digital Media is going great!

As I sit at my computer, surrounded by technology and immersed in the world of digital media, I can't help but feel grateful for the path that has led me here.

Growing up in Silicon Valley, I was exposed to the ever-evolving landscape of technology from a young age. Google University was practically in my backyard, and the world's tech giants were just a stone's throw away. But at the same time, I was also surrounded by the arts. My family members either worked in the tech industry or pursued creative passions such as music and art.

It was no surprise then, that I found myself drawn to the world of digital media. In middle school, I tinkered with coding and web design, and as I grew older, I enrolled in more focused classes. As I near graduation with an A.S. in Digital Media, I've found my niche.

Of course, my journey hasn't been without its challenges. As I learned to navigate the complex world of graphics, photography, and content creation, there were times when I felt overwhelmed. But every setback has only served to fuel my determination to master my craft.

And I know that my skills will continue to evolve. After all, the world of digital media is constantly changing and evolving, just like the tech landscape of my hometown. But that excites me - the potential to create something new, explore new mediums and platforms, and continue to grow as both a digital artist and a person.

As I look to the future, I know that the world of digital media is where I belong. And I can't wait to see where this journey takes me next.
